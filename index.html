Who I am

Strategy, insight,
and a vision
driven by impact

I started my marketing career in London in 2016 by founding my own agency — earning a UK Tier 1 Graduate Entrepreneur endorsement, and subsequently obtaining Innovator Founder and Start-up status. Since then, I've spent nine years navigating diverse sectors, from luxury and beauty to Web3 and fintech.

My approach is strictly end-to-end. I handle the research, map out the strategy, and drive the execution. Rather than getting lost in the data, I use analytics to inform clear, impactful communication.

Supported by an MSc in Digital Marketing and a prestigious UK professional qualification in strategic management and leadership (CMI Level 7 Diploma), I specialise in making complex concepts accessible and turning strategic goals into tangible results.
